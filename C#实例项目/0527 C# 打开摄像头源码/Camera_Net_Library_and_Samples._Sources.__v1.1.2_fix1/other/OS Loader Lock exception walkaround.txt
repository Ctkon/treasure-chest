OS Loader Lock exception walkaround

http://social.msdn.microsoft.com/forums/en-US/gametechnologiesgeneral/thread/5eea42dc-95e6-47e2-b992-2e48291e6bf7/

In VS2005 go to Debug/Exceptions. Open the Managed Debugging Assistants node on the tree, scroll down till you find LoaderLock and uncheck it.
